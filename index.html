Hi! I'm Paayal Zaveri, a journalist from the San Francisco Bay Area. I'm currently a master's candidate at the University of California, Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism. I am a writer and data reporter, with experience in online, radio and broadcast storytelling. I'm also learning how to code. This past summer, I was a NBC/AAJA fellow at CNBC, where I wrote about markets and financial news. My work has been published in CNBC, NBC Asian America, KQED Public Radio, San Francisco Public Press and Richmond Confidential.
